About JobPortal

We are on a mission to bridge the gap between talented professionals and companies that need them — making hiring simple, transparent and accessible for everyone.

Mission & Vision

What drives us every single day

Our Mission

To empower students and professionals across India by connecting them with the right job opportunities through a fast, modern and bias-free platform. We believe everyone deserves access to great career opportunities regardless of background or location.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted job portal by 2027 — where companies of all sizes can find the right talent instantly, and job seekers can discover roles that match not just their skills but their ambitions and values.

Our Values

Transparency, inclusivity and innovation are at the heart of everything we build. We treat every job seeker and company as a partner — not a number — and continuously improve based on real user feedback.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 at St. John College of Engineering and Management, Mumbai — JobPortal started as a student project to solve a real problem: the disconnect between talented graduates and companies actively hiring. Today it serves thousands of users across India.

Why Choose JobPortal?

Everything you need to find or fill a role — in one place

Smart Job Search

Search and filter jobs by role, category, location and salary. Find relevant opportunities in seconds, not hours.

Real Company Profiles

Every job listing shows detailed company info — size, industry, headquarters and website — so you know exactly who you're applying to.

One-Click Apply

Apply to multiple positions across multiple companies with a single click. Track all your applications in your profile dashboard.

For Companies Too

Companies can register, post jobs, receive applications, and select or reject candidates — all from their own dedicated dashboard.

Live Job Market

Jobs are fetched in real time from our database and The Muse API — giving you access to thousands of real openings across the globe.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design that works perfectly on your phone, tablet or desktop. Apply from anywhere, anytime.
